The Blockchain Availability and Costs Planning Tool.

Go to BPPT

Plan your Blockchain-based Application.

This tool relies on the Hyperledger Fabric main components.

Improve your Availability Indexes.

Select an endorsement policy that matches your applications.

Aim at the Cost x Benefit Relationship.

Compare the Deployment Prices in both Public and Private Infrastructures.

We have modeled the Hyperledger Fabric environment through a Multilevel CTMC in Mercury Tool and exported this model to Wolfram Mathematica.

Then, we have used the State Diagrams package and obtained the availability expression that corresponds to this CTMC, and moved it to Python.

By using Django Framework and Plotly library we could reach an awesome tool that can saves you time and money on availability and costs planning.
