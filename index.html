Carlos Melo

MSc., Informatics Center, 2016. Lattes CV


Received his Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in 2013 and his Masters' in 2016. He was a professor with a cellist link at the Alpha Faculdade in Systems Analysis and Development course between 2018 and 2019. From 2015 to 2017 he worked on a research project signed by Motorola in the scope of R&D&I.

His areas of interest include performance evaluation, dependability, energy consumption, capacity planning, Petri nets, and formal models, covering the areas of cloud computing, sustainable data centers, networks, and distributed systems, as well as blockchain and its technologies.
